About Me:

Hello! Thank you for visiting my page. I am a recent graduate of Codeup, an immersive 16 week program where I learned full-stack LAMP + J web development. I am excited about finding a new career in software and web development. I am looking to apply my knowledge to a team environment that emphasizes mentorship, and excited to learn new things. Please take a look at my resume, and check out my portfolio which contains some of the projects that I have completed.

Originally from Tennessee, I have called San Antonio, TX home for the last 17 years. My career began in telecommunications when I enlisted in the Air Force where I was a Telecommunications Technician while stationed at Kelly AFB. I used this experience to work in various roles with Worldcom and Verizon providing telecommuncations support for clients nationwide. Prior to enrolling in Codeup I was General Manager at Cambridge Auto Center, and was responsible for day to day operations of the facility and managed 20 employees. I also host a live call-in radio show every Saturday on a local radio station, 1200AM WOAI, called Under the Hood which is focused on automotive repair and maintenance.

My hobbies include reading, golfing, traveling, attending concerts and music festivals and an occasional game of rugby with old teammates. My favorite hobby is spending time with my family.
