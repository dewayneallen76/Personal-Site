About Me:

Hello! Thank you for visiting my page. I am a recent graduate of Codeup, an immersive 16 week program where I learned full-stack LAMP + J web development. I am excited about finding a new career in software and web development. I am looking to apply my full stack development skills on a team that supports growth for clients and employees alike. Please take a look at my resume, and check out my portfolio which contains some of the projects that I have completed.

My career began in telecommunications when I enlisted in the Air Force where I was a Telecommunications Technician while stationed at Kelly AFB. I used this experience to work in various roles with Worldcom and Verizon providing telecommuncations support for clients nationwide. Prior to enrolling in Codeup I was General Manager at Cambridge Auto Center, and was responsible for day to day operations of the facility and managed 20 employees. I also host a live, call-in radio show every Saturday on a local radio station called Under the Hood which is focused on automotive repair and maintenance which can be heard here.

I enjoy reading, golfing, traveling, attending concerts and music festivals, an occasional game of rugby with old teammates and spending time with my family.
